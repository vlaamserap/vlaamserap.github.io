Uberdope @ Once upon a Festival, Laarne

13 april 2012

4 stages, 2 days and more than 70 artists and bands. Belgium’s first mind expanding – Tjingel – Tjangel – Boom – Bloom – World – Festival, supported by lots of art, visuals and performances. Location near the old castle of Laarne.

-Soul stage ft. World music, reggae, dub gipsy, balkan, (post)rock, ska, elektroswing, eclectic, , turntablism, electronica.
-Body stage ft. Dubstep, ragga, jungle, drum and bass, acid, core, breaks, rave.
-Mind stage ft. Electronica, Minimal, Techno, House.
-Spirit stage ft. Experimental, electronica, upcoming talent, ambient, psychedelic, chillout, IDM, weird stuff, cinema.

Locatie: Kasteel van Laarne, 9270 Laarne, Belgium
Tijdstip: 13th & 14th of april // The festival for your body, mind and soul.
E-mailadres: info@onceuponafestival.be
Website: http://www.onceuponafestival.be
